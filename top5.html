TOP 5 BEST THINGS IN THE PENCIL CASE


  1. pencil
    • you can write with it
    • usually made with wood
  2. eraser
    • can erase things
    • squishy
  3. highlighter
    • highlights things
    • makes the paper look colorful
  4. pencil sharpener
    • it sharpens the pencil
    • allows for extra pencil use
  5. pen
    • unerasable writing
    • doesn't need resharpening
